Blog vs Digital Garden

  • Home page of blogs is a list sorted date-wise. Digital garden has a curated home page.
    • Blog can have a curated home page.
  • Blog is performative writing. Digital garden in more like a broadcast log.
  • Digital garden is more search based.
    • Andy Matuschak notes do not have search.
  • Digital gardens can devolve to unorganized mess.
