A CT scan for COVID merits a word of caution

by Lancelot Pinto, Consultant respirologist, Hinduja National hospital, Mumbai

  • CT scan is less accurate than RT PCR in diagnosis of Covid (70% vs 62%).
  • CT scan provides no advantage over pulse oximeter in prognosis.
  • CT radiation is a proven cause of cancer
  • CT done in a closed air conditioned room. It is an environment that boosts spreading of virus.
  • Use CT if patient has symptoms of Covid and 2 consecutive RTPCR turns negative.
