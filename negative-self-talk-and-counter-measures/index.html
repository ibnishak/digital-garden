Negative self-talk and counter measures

  • Personalizing: Everybody is mad at me, They do not like me etc
    • Is it factual or my interpretation of the event?
    • Is there solid evidence to support my view?
    • What alternative explanations are there for the event?
  • Filtering: See only negative aspects of a day or event
    • Interstitial journal: Keep a record of everything you do n few words, immediately. At the end of day, you can see the positives too.
  • Catastrophising: Anticipate the worst.
    • Ask yourselves: How likely is this to happen?
    • Distinguish between uncomfortable outcomes vs. catastrophic outcomes.
    • Can a stalled train actually stay stuck for decades? And, if it doesn't, wouldn’t you still be okay in the long run?
  • Polarizing: It’s the feeling that you have to be perfect or you’re a total failure
    • If you don’t do something perfectly, reassure yourself that you’re only human. You’re allowed to make choices that cater to your needs at the moment.
