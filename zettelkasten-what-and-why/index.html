Zettelkasten - What and Why

Zettelkasten is a workflow used by Niklas Luhmann

A word about zettelkasten is warranted here. To me, zettelkasten is a workflow, rather than something tangible. It involves 3 steps

  1. Add interesting snippets to a temporary folder. Like inbox in GTD workflow.
  2. Move notes from folder 1 to folder 2. This involves 2 conditions
    1. Any snippets should be rewritten in your own words and elaborated. Quotes should also accompany your thoughts on that.
    2. Link to other notes. Links could be to a more general note, more specific note or to a note parallel to current note. Categorization of notes is highly discouraged.
  3. Move notes from folder 2 to folder 3 by coalescing small notes to a larger one. Folder 3 is called evergreen notes by Sohnke Ahren. It could also be your blog posts, personal knowledge base or webpage.

Why

  1. Avoid Collector's fallacy
  2. Knowledge to Wisdom
