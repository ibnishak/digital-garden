Zettelkasten - What and Why

Zettelkasten is a workflow used by Niklas Luhmann. It is popularized in recent times by Sohnke Ahren

A word about zettelkasten is warranted here. To me, zettelkasten is a workflow, rather than something tangible. It involves 3 steps

  1. Collect interesting snippets, ideas, passages etc. It is like inbox in GTD workflow.
  2. Move notes from folder 1 to folder 2. This involves 2 conditions
    1. Any snippets should be rewritten in your own words and elaborated. Quotes should also accompany your thoughts on the same. See Necessity of writing notes in your own words.
    2. Link to other notes. See. Cross referencing in Zettelkasten. Categorization of notes is highly discouraged.
  3. Coalescing small notes to a larger one. It could also be your blog posts, personal knowledge base or webpage.

Why

  1. Avoid Collector's fallacy
  2. Knowledge to Wisdom
