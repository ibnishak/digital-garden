Yes or no

In a press conference, a journalist questioned posed a diificult question to a Politician. The politician, in true political style, answered the question in a long winding manner.

The journalist, perhaps an impatient rookie, stressed on a yes or no, for his question. And politician continued to add an explanation instead of a direct yes or no. At which stage the journalist lost his cool and shouted - "I need a yes or no".

The politician took a moment and replied, "Every question cannot have a yes or no response".

"Yes it can. Every question can have a yes or no - if asked in the right manner, which I have", responded the journalist.

"Alright. In that case - have you stopped beating your wife?"
