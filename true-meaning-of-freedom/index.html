True meaning of freedom

In 1959 (or 1960?) during the UN General Assembly annual meeting, Eisenhower first went up to give his speech. He said:

"Americans know the meaning of true freedom. If someone went to the centre of the Times Square and shouted "The US President is a fool", he would go away scot-free and maybe even gain a few supporters."

On hearing this, Khurschev, whose turn was after Eisenhower's, said:

"Well I don't know about freedom, but if someone went to the centre of the Red Square and shouted "The US President is a fool", he would not only go away scot-free, but even be rewarded with state honors! "
