Workflow - Reading and Notetaking

  • Create highlights and notes. Prefer writing notes on your own words summarising every few paragraphs. See Necessity of writing notes in your own words
    • For Ebooks, use Moonreader in Android and Foliate in Linux. See Workflow - Overview
  • Expand, connect and tag notes. If possible, categorise it.
  • Consider how you would bring that ideas to your life
    • See Collector's fallacy
  • Schedule and Track those changes
