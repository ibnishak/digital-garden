Necessity of debates

Polarized opinions mean topics matter enough for people to care. Healthy debate contributes to identifying weaknesses and fueling growth, while indifference would let it stagnate and die. - Sarah Dayan

a. Not all topics deserve a debate. There are black and white matters.
b. Not everyone debates in good faith. Healthy debate requires honesty about one's own failures and humility to reflect. In reality, that rarely happens.
c. Raging debates confuse young and inexperienced.
d. People who take sides without understanding nuances of each arguments end up pushing for extremes.
