Necessity of debates

Polarized opinions mean topics matter enough for people to care. Healthy debate contributes to identifying weaknesses and fueling growth, while indifference would let it stagnate and die. - Sarah Dayan

  • Not all topics deserve a debate. There are black and white matters.
  • Not everyone debates in good faith. Healthy debate requires honesty about one's own failures and humility to reflect. In reality, that rarely happens.
  • Raging debates confuse young and inexperienced.
  • People who take sides without understanding nuances of each arguments end up pushing for extremes.
