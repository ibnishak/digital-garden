A tourist goes to Ireland

A tourist goes to Ireland to visit family and walks into a pub with his cousin.

After he orders his drink an elderly man shout "47!" and everyone laughs. A younger man yells "19!" and everyone laughs again.

The tourist turns to his cousin and says "What is everyone doing?"

"We Irish have so many classic jokes we associate them with numbers. Give it a try."

The tourist looks around, clears his throat and shouts "149!"

The whole room explodes, everyone is laughing, some fall off their seats and all have tears running down their face. The tourist turns to his cousin and says "What happened?"

Between laughs the cousin says: "We haven't heard that one before!"
